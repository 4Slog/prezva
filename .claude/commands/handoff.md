# /handoff — Hand off to Claude Desktop for a decision

When you need a senior decision, architectural input, or hit something uncertain:

1. Stop what you are doing — do not guess or proceed
2. Write a clear summary to `/tmp/prezva-handoff.md`:
   - What task you were working on
   - What you found or what the problem is
   - What options you see
   - What you need decided
3. Output: "🔔 HANDOFF NEEDED — Claude Desktop please review /tmp/prezva-handoff.md"
4. Wait for instruction before continuing
